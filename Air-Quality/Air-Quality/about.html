Welcome to AirQualityIN

AirQualityIN provides real-time air quality updates in India, focusing on the impact of air pollution on health and the environment. Access air quality data, understand pollution sources, and learn about measures being implemented to combat pollution in India.

Mission

The mission of AirQualityIN is to raise awareness and provide data-driven insights on air pollution in India. Our goal is to empower individuals, communities, and policymakers to take action for cleaner air and a healthier environment for everyone.

Things to Explore

  • Real-time Air Quality Data: Access current AQI and pollutant levels for various Indian regions.
  • Causes of Air Pollution: Learn about India's unique pollution sources such as vehicle emissions, crop stubble burning, and industrial emissions.
  • Measures of Air Pollution: Discover India's initiatives like the National Clean Air Programme (NCAP) to improve air quality.
  • Comparison to Other Countries: See how India's air quality compares globally.
